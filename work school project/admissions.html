University Admissions

Unity University seeks students who demonstrate academic excellence and leadership potential aligned with Ethiopia's development goals.

1. Undergraduate Requirements

For Ethiopian School Leaving Certificate (ESLCE) holders:

  • Completed application form (500 Birr non-refundable fee)
  • Original ESLCE results (Minimum 2.0 GPA)
  • Two passport photos
  • Copy of Kebele ID/Birth Certificate
  • Motivation letter (300 words in English)

International students: Equivalent qualifications with Ethiopian Embassy authentication

2. Entrance Examination

All applicants must take:

  • Undergraduates: General aptitude test (English, Math, Logic) - March 15, 2024
  • Postgraduates: Program-specific exams - Dates vary by department

3. Interview Process

Shortlisted candidates undergo:

  • Faculty panel interview (for competitive programs)
  • English proficiency assessment
  • For MBA: Work experience verification

4. Admission Decisions

Notifications issued within 3 weeks after exams via:

  • SMS to Ethiopian mobile numbers
  • Official university portal
  • Physical collection letters (for rural applicants)
