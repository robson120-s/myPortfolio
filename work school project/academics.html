Our Academic Programs

Unity University offers internationally recognized programs grounded in Ethiopian educational excellence.

Undergraduate Programs

Four-year degree programs with Ethiopian and global perspectives:

  • Business Administration (Accounting, Management, Marketing)
  • Computer Science & Information Technology
  • Economics & Development Studies
  • Law & International Relations
  • Engineering (Civil, Electrical, Software)
  • Ethiopian Languages & Literature (Amharic, Afaan Oromo)

Graduate Programs

Advanced studies addressing Ethiopia's development needs:

  • MBA (Regular & Executive)
  • MSc in Development Economics
  • MA in Ethiopian Studies
  • MSc in Information Systems
  • Postgraduate Diploma

Continuing Education

Professional development programs aligned with Ethiopian labor market needs:

  • Project Management (PMI aligned)
  • Accounting for Diplomats
  • Entrepreneurship & Business
  • Preparatory Program for Ethiopian University Entrance
