About Unity University

Mission & Vision


Our Mission

To deliver quality education through innovative teaching, research, and community services that address Ethiopia's socio-economic challenges while maintaining global standards.

Our Vision

To be the leading private higher education institution in Ethiopia by 2030, producing competent graduates who contribute to national development and global knowledge.

Core Values

  • Academic Excellence: Rigorous standards in teaching and research
  • Innovation: Fostering creative problem-solving
  • Ethiopian Identity: Preserving cultural heritage
  • Integrity: Ethical practices in all operations
